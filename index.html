  • Venue & Weather

    Both the ceremony and reception location are open air and close to the beach with temperatures averaging 70-80 degrees. Typical weather is sunny with a light breeze.

  • Suggested Attire

    We recommend lightweight and flowy attire that keeps you cool and comfortable. Sensible footwear is recommended as the path to our ceremony site is a bit uneven and rocky. Your boy Sean loves to dress up but your favorite collared or Aloha shirt is perfectly fine too!

    If you're flying in, we highly recommned renting a car to explore the area. Parking will be available on-site.

    We're arranging a room block at the Marriott with a special rate for the weekend of the wedding. Be sure to book early as rooms are limited! There are several additional accommodation options in the immediate area.
